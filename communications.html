Communicating

I believe that proper and timely communication and dissemination of research results is equally important to conducting core research activities. Hence, I am highly active as a communicator—organising conferences and workshops, giving invited talks at various venues (both for research audiences and the general public), writing blog posts and tutorials, and participating in webinars.

In line with this, I am very happy to receive invitations for talks, podcasts, event co-organising, and generally any mode of communicating which is listed below. Please see my contact page if you have such an offer!
